What is Heartful.ly?

Heartful.ly is a wedding registry for charitable giving.

We partner with amazing non-profit organizations in the U.S. and around the world so you can support real projects in need of funding. Think: health care for the homeless, sports organizations for underserved youth, or access to clean water for entire villages.
