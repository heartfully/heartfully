How does it work?

Heartful.ly is a wedding registry for generous couples to share their happily ever after with the world.

Step 1:
Create your registry

Choose a unique URL, share some basic information about your big day, and upload a few photos.

Step 2:
Select a project to support

Browse vetted charitable projects by region or issue and select your favorite one.

Step 3:
See the impact!

After your wedding, you and your guests will receive an update on how the project is helping people.
